Tofu

Tofu was my dog and he had a lot of problems. But I really loved him and he was a great companion. I miss him every day.

He was born in the Hague and I was told he had a tough upbringing in a puppy mill. Later I learned that developmental issues show up in dogs around 1 year old when they go through puperty. When he was a puppy you wouldn't suspect that something was wrong but soon after his first birthday he started attacking dogs in the park and behaving irraticaly.

Tofu would jump into various boxes and bins of clothes and fall asleep while I was working from home. Due to some pain and unknown history when we got him at 6 months old we needed to help him relax and sleep more. Initially we bought him some Thundershirts and eventually we got him some headbands that would help him sleep better and relax.

He was never really scared of fireworks to my surprise. I remember even walking him around the neighborhood during New Year's Eve in the middle of Centraal Amsterdam and he didn't seem to care at all about the bombs going off around us. Before he got anxious and scared of other dogs, I would take him to a local park in Amsterdam Noord, take him off-lease (even though I wasn't supposed to) and play fetch with him for the longest time.

Tofu knows hot to sit, lay, spin, roll over, "touch" and come between my legs and walk along with me. He could also play dead, fetch and it was really easy to teach him new tricks. He would pick them up from pretty much a single sesssion.
